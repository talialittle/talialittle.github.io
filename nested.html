What a Gymnast Needs for Competition

Each event may require different essentials to help a gymnast train. For example, Bars requires gymnasts to have grips. Other essentials are...

  • Chalk or Powder

  • Water

  • Body Tape

  • Tiger Balm

  • What gymnasts might need for...
    Beam:
    -wrist guards, chalk, body tape

  • What gymnasts might need for...
    Floor:
    -body tape, music, wrist guards, ankle braces, chalk

  • What gymnasts might need for...
    Vault:
    -tape to line the runway, wrist guards, chalk
